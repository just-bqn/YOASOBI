Who is YOASOBI?

YOASOBI is a J-Pop duo, formed in 2019, composed of Vocaloid producer Ayase and singer-songwriter Ikura. With the slogan "Novel into music", the group has released numerous original songs, based on short stories and light novels.

What is this website?

This website was created as a small fan project by me, _bqn. Here, you can discover the discography of YOASOBI, including Spotify and YouTube links to all of their releases, as well as the lyrics from Genius.
